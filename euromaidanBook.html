Euromaidan

12.2019
Drum leaf, 40 pages
10.25" x 8"
A book about Euromaidan, the 93 days of revolution which rocked Ukraine from November 21st, 2013 until February 22nd, 2014. The book features statements from protestors involved in Euromaidan: a transcription of Volodymyr Parasiukâ€™s address to the nation given at Maidan Nezalezhnosti on February 21st, 2014, and an article reflecting on Euromaidan by Mustafa Nayam, whose Facebook post is often credited with starting the protests. I also included an explanation and a timeline of Euromaidan, which I wrote based off my own research. I paired this copy with found photographs, and I created an illustration of a statue found at the location of the protests for the cover.
