About Me

Hi, my name is Andriana Levytsky. I am a junior pursuing a BFA in Communication Design at the Sam Fox School of Design & Visual Arts at Washington University in St. Louis with minors in Art and Art History. I am originally from New Jersey, but my family is Ukrainian, which has had a large influence on my work. I am also interested in the different ways that people interact and interpret art and design, and how this interaction affects the meaning of the work.
When I am not in studio, I like exploring different music venues / performers and hanging out with my friends.
