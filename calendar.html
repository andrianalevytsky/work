Sam Fox Calendar

10.2019
Print media
36" x 24"
A double-sided poster advertising exhibitions, lectures, tours, screenings and other events put on by the Sam Fox School of Design & Visual Arts. The images, logo, and copy featured on the poster were all provided and required to be integrated into the design. The interaction between bold type and images, and the subsequent diagonals and shapes created by that interaction engage the viewer and help link the visual language of the two sides of the poster.
