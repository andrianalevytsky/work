How it's Made

11.2019
Accordion, 10 pages
5" x 7"
A ten-page book illustrating where natural gas comes from and how it is made. The book uses a limited color pallet and relies solely on images made from ink drawings and scans of dirt in order to relay information. The vertical orientation of the pages references the motion of drilling down into the ground for natural gas.
