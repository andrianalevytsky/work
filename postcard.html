Rhythms of Stl

12.2019
Print media
4" x 6"
A series of twelve postcards, a stamp, and packaging highlighting St. Louis, particularly its diverse music scene and history, created in celebration of Missouri’s upcoming bicentennial in 2021. Each postcard focuses on either a different genre or a person or place prevalent to St. Louis’ music scene. The bold colors and shapes are meant to visually mimic the many sounds and rhythms of the postcard’s subjects, and the packaging of the postcards was inspired by the packaging of records and record sleeves.
