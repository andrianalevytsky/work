Touch

11.2019
Silkscreen on linen
Twin sheets
A twin bed with screen prints on the sheets. The prints are line drawings based on photographs found on my phone, computer, and camera in which people were touching each other in some way. It is loosely color coded based on the touchâ€™s level of intimacy. The way that the drawings are printed on the sheets requires viewers to get into bed or move the sheets in some way to see them. The work explores different kinds of touch and the interaction that results from touching, both via its imagery and the interaction required from the viewer to fully see the piece.
